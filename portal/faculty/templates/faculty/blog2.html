Sample Cover Letter

To The Recruitment Team,
Dalberg Global Development Advisors

My name is Devansh Jain, a third-year undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Sciences in Mathematics and Computing at IIT Kharagpur. My interests lie in social impact consulting, strategy, and venture capital. I am writing to express my strong interest in the Dalberg Undergraduate Internship Program and to convey my enthusiasm for contributing to your esteemed organization's mission of driving inclusive growth through strategic advisory services.

I am eager to bring my skills and passion for creating a positive societal impact to the dynamic and purpose-driven community at Dalberg. My academic journey in Mathematics and Engineering entrepreneurship has equipped me with a robust foundation in strategic thinking, analytical capabilities, and a keen understanding of private sector dynamics.

I have actively sought opportunities to apply these skills in real-world settings, most notably during my internship as a Strategy Intern at Feeding India by Zomato, where I primarily worked on the food recovery project of the Global FoodBanking Network impacting millions of farmers to reduce their farm loss by 10-15% and increasing their incomes substantially. I have also led public-private partnerships addressing malnutrition and had the opportunity to work with the Offices of Hon'ble Governors and Hon'ble Chief Ministers, alongside multiple in-house innovation initiatives to revamp the food ecosystem in the nation further honing my strategic skills.

To complement my academic and practical experiences, I have actively engaged in coursework and initiatives that align with Dalberg's mission. In the past, I have worked as a Research Associate at Cambridge Judge Business School, focusing on strengthening Micro, Small, and medium Enterprises, and as a Strategy and Marketing Intern at Blu Ocean Private Limited, managing their celebrity collaborations including Robert Downey Jr. I have also worked at the prestigious Scheller College of Business, Georgia Tech in the finance domain.

Furthermore, my ability to collaborate across diverse sectors has been demonstrated through my involvement in the Entrepreneurship Cell at IIT Kharagpur. I facilitated the highest-ever funding(1.2 Cr+ INR) to startups and organized India’s largest Business Model competition, Empresario. Besides that, I have also excelled in numerous case study competitions such as the prestigious Indian Case Challenge, EY Cafta Case Championship, HSBC Case Competition, and HackOn with Amazon 3.0 where I worked seamlessly with stakeholders from public, private, and philanthropic sectors to achieve common goals. I honed my managerial competency & team bonding skills and used my communication prowess to forge global associations and eventually make Global Entrepreneurship Summit (GES) India’s first Carbon-Neutral E-Summit in 2021.

Dalberg's commitment to leveraging private sector strategy for the greater good deeply resonates with me, and I am particularly drawn to the organization's global approach to addressing pressing challenges. I am confident that my analytical mindset, strategic acumen, and passion for inclusive growth align well with Dalberg's values and objectives.

Thank you for considering my application, and I look forward to the possibility of contributing to Dalberg's impactful work.

Yours sincerely,
Devansh Jain
